3. How we use your data

We use your data for one purpose: to provide you with the Lumren experience. Specifically:

  • To build your signal portrait and soul memory within the app
  • To generate companion messages that are specific to you
  • To detect patterns and behavioral loops in real time
  • To produce your Weekly Soul Letter and breakthrough detections
  • To restore your data if you change devices, via encrypted backup only

We do not use your data to train AI models. Your journal entries, voice tone signals, and soul memory are never passed to AI providers in a way that allows training. All prompts are processed in real time and not retained.

We do not build advertising profiles. We have no advertising business and no advertising partners.

We do not use your data for research without your explicit, informed, separately obtained consent.

5. Your rights

You have the following rights over your data at all times:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to deletion: Delete all your data permanently from Settings, then Your Data, then Delete Everything. This is irreversible and takes under 3 seconds. You can also contact us at [email protected] and we will process the deletion within 30 days.
  • Right to portability: Export your soul memory, journal entries, and signal history from Settings, then Your Data, then Export
  • Right to correction: Update or correct your account information at any time
  • Right to object: Object to any processing you did not intend to consent to

For users in the European Economic Area, UK, or California, these rights apply under GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA respectively. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

6. Data security

  • All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • All data in transit uses TLS 1.3
  • Your soul memory is encrypted on your device before it is ever transmitted
  • We use row-level security on our database so no query can access another user's data
  • HealthKit data is processed on-device only and never transmitted to our servers
  • We conduct regular security reviews

No system is completely secure. If we become aware of a security breach affecting your data, we will notify you within 72 hours.

7. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • All soul memory, journal entries, and signal history are deleted immediately from your device
  • Server-side data is purged within 30 days
  • Backup copies are destroyed within 90 days
  • Anonymised, non-identifying aggregate statistics may be retained

Signal Twin fingerprints are anonymised behavioral hashes with no personal identifiers and are automatically deleted after 7 days regardless of account status.

8. Children

Lumren is not intended for users under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 17. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at [email protected] and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We will notify you of material changes to this policy via email and an in-app notification at least 14 days before changes take effect. Continued use of the app after that point constitutes acceptance. If you disagree with the changes, you can delete your account before they take effect.
