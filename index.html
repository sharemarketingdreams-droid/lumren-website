Questions

Do I need a credit card to start?+
No. Sign up and you're in. We only ask for payment details on day 3 if you want to continue. Cancel before then and we charge you nothing.
Does the heart scan work without a wearable?+
Yes. Your rear camera detects micro-changes in blood flow through your fingertip, the same technique used in clinical pulse oximeters. 30 seconds is enough to read HRV, stress, and recovery score.
What devices does Lumren work on?+
iOS and Android. Heart rate sync also works with Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, and Wear OS devices if you want continuous monitoring between scans.
Is my health data private?+
Your biometric and emotional data is encrypted on your device before it moves anywhere. We don't sell it, share it, or use it to train models. It's yours.
Can I cancel at any time?+
One tap in the app. No confirmation loops, no email chains, no chat required. Instant.
