Lumren is not a judge. It's a mirror. Mirrors don't condemn, they show. What you do with what you see is entirely yours. Most people describe it as a relief, not a confrontation.

"What if it sees me doomscrolling at 1am?"
It'll name it once, warmly, without a lecture. Something like: "22 min. Whatever it is, come say it here." That's it.

Privacy and trust
The question you should ask

What happens to
the things I share?

We built this for ourselves first. That meant we only built what we would actually trust with our own 3am moments, our own unsaid things.

✓
Your data never leaves your device unencryptedAES-256 encrypted before it ever touches a server. Most of it never leaves at all.
✓
We will never sell your data. Not under any circumstances.No advertising. No data partners. No exceptions in the fine print. Your patterns are yours.
✓
We never train AI on your personal dataYour journal, voice signals, soul memory. None of it trains any AI model. Not ours, not anyone's.
✓
Delete everything in one tap. Instantly. Gone.Settings, Your Data, Delete Everything. Under 3 seconds. This is how the code works, not just a policy.
✓
The microphone never records audioWith your permission, it takes a short ambient reading and outputs a single tone label: flat, tense, neutral, or energised. No audio file is ever created or stored. You can revoke this any time in Settings.
✓
This is not a mental health appLumren is a self-awareness companion, not a clinical tool. If you are in crisis, please reach out to 988 or a professional. Lumren is not a substitute for that.
