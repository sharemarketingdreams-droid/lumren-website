Privacy and trust

what happens to the
things you share?

🔒
Your data never leaves your device unencrypted
AES-256 encrypted before it ever touches a server. Most of it never leaves at all.
✕
We will never sell your data. Not ever.
No advertising. No data partners. No exceptions in the fine print. Your patterns are yours.
◎
We never train AI on your personal data
Your journal, voice signals, soul memory. None of it trains any AI model. Not ours, not anyone's.
⌫
Delete everything in one tap. Instantly.
Settings → Your Data → Delete Everything. Under 3 seconds. This is how the code works, not just a policy.
🎙
The microphone never records audio
Takes a short ambient reading and outputs a tone label: flat, tense, neutral, or energised. No audio file ever created or stored.
⚠
This is not a mental health app
Lumren is a self-awareness companion, not a clinical tool. If you are in crisis, please reach out to 988 or a professional.
